TORONTO

Queen Street West

& Yorkville

GOING TO TORONTO WITH MY FRIENDS HAS ALWAYS

BEEN A GO-TO ESCAPE FROM THE BORING QUITE LIFE WE

HAVE BACK AT HOME. A QUICK 45 MINUTE TRAIN LATER,

WE BECOME OUR MOST CONFIDENT SELVES. DRESSING UP,

GOING TO THE CITY AND OUT TO EAT AT RESTAURANTS LIKE

SOFIA'S, CANOE, JACOB'S, THE CHASE OR SOTTO SOTTO

THIS YEAR HAVE BEEN SOME OF MY BEST MEMORIES.

PLANS LIKE THESE BRINGS ALL MY FRIENDS TOGETHER

WHERE WE'RE ALL GLAMMED UP AND HAVING A GOOD TIME

IS AN OPPORTUNITY I DON'T WANT TO EVER MISS.
